fn div_32 #

fn div_32(hi u32, lo u32, y u32) (u32, u32)

--- Full-width divide --- div_32 returns the quotient and remainder of (hi, lo) divided by y: quo = (hi, lo)/y, rem = (hi, lo)%y with the dividend bits' upper half in parameter hi and the lower half in parameter lo.
div_32 panics for y == 0 (division by zero) or y <= hi (quotient overflow).
